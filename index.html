Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are known for their ability to change colors.
  • Chameleons can change their skin color with a special layer of skin that contain guanine crystals. Chameleons change colors by changing the space between the crystals which causes the wavelength of light being reflected to change, which in turn causes a chameleon's skin to look different.
  • Chameleons change colors to bend in to their environment to avoid predators and help sneak up on prey.



Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas are actually a type of bear.
  • Pandas live mainly in China and some of it's bordering contries.
  • Pandas can eat a lot of bamboo.



Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  • Cicadas can hibernate for up to 17 years.
  • Cicadas can be found in every continent except Antarctica.
  • Cicadas can produce a racket of up to 110 decibels with special abdomens.



Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are very common in cities.
  • Pigeons are incredibly intelligent.
  • Pigeons are highly social animals.
